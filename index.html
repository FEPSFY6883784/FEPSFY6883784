The impact early diagnosis has had

Early detection of cancer prevents metastasis from happening at a quicker rate (Pashayan, N. & Pharoah, 2020). Early interventions are primarily aimed at populations at higher risk of developing cancer, often based on age or the presence of symptoms. These individuals are referred to hospitals, which identifies the presence of cancer and determines its stage. Cancer is classified into four main stages, indicating its location and whether it has spread to surrounding tissue. The higher the stage, the further the cancer has spread.
